PL-300T00: Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Course Overview

The PL-300T00: Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst course is designed to equip learners with comprehensive knowledge and skills to analyze data with Power BI. It covers a wide range of topics, starting with getting started with Microsoft Data Analytics, where learners are introduced to the concepts of data analytics within the Microsoft ecosystem and the fundamentals of using Power BI, including hands-on labs.As learners progress, they explore modules on getting data, transforming data, and data modeling using Power BI Desktop. The course emphasizes practical skills in data cleaning, DAX, report creation, data analysis, and performance optimization.Advanced modules teach participants about creating dashboards, applying row-level security, and enhancing reports for usability. The course also covers managing workspaces, datasets, and sharing content within the Power BI service. By the end of the course, learners will have hands-on experience with Power BI Desktop and Power BI Service, enabling them to transform raw data into compelling data visualizations and reports for insightful business decision-making.
